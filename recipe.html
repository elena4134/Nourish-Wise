Telur Dadar Sayur

Low-Cost and Nutritious Breakfast!

How to Make Telur Dadar

  1. Beat eggs with chopped veggies.
  2. Add salt/pepper and mix well.
  3. Pour into heated pan, cook both sides.
  4. Serve hot with rice or bread.

Basic Fried Rice

Turn leftovers into a delicious, satisfying meal!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce, oil

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, fry egg and veggies.
  2. Add rice and soy sauce, stir well.
  3. Cook for 2-3 minutes and serve hot.
